Hardcore Rap is aggression part of hiphop, whether in the lyrical subject matter, the hard, driving beats, the noisy sampling and production. Hardcore rap is tough, streetwise, intense, and often menacing. This type of hip hop originated from the East Coast in th late 80s. hip hop scene. The most common things that hardcore artists talk about are sex, nudity, partying, drugs, profanity, police brutality, racism, personal conflicts, consciousness, social issues and the harsh surroundings of the rapper's environment.

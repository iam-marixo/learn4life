Hip Hop

Hip-hop is best known for its impact on music in the form of the musical genre of the same name. It's said that DJ Kool Herc a Jamaican-American DJ is credited for the beginning of hiphop music in the 1970s in The Bronx, New York City. HipHop has came a very long way in the past 43 years. It went from 6 hiphop artists to so many you can remember their names.

Before Hip Hop

Hip Hop wasn't money-motivated; it was the product of a bunch of kids from a bunch of different places trying to talk to each other and explain their story through a different type of way. HipHop teaches us more about like more than any other music genre. It covers vast topics like hardships and love. It makes people feel cocky, confident and proud of the themselves.
